DevCon Community Technology Initiative

We are a group volunteers, organized by DevCon Philippines, whose mission is to design and develop robust, radically simple, and rapidly deployable solutions to help solve the COVID-19 Pandemic in the Philippines and beyond.

Our Solutions are aimed to

Be User-Friendly and for Mass Adoptation

We design solutions that are user friendly and can be adopted quickly across several platforms.

Be Managed by Proper Authorities

Our platforms will be managed and updated by the proper authorities such as health, government, and disaster relief officials.

Be User-Initiated and Self-Sovereign

Our platform allows information to be user-initiated and self-sovereign meaning that there is better control over user data.

Protect the Privacy of Individuals

We protect the data of individuals in line with the Data Privacy Act.

Be OPENSOURCED as ASF license

Our solutions will be open-sourced with Apache Software Foundation to accelerate transparency, reliability and collaboration.
